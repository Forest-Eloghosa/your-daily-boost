About This Project

Your Daily Boost is a simple wellness tracker designed to help you log your moods and receive motivational quotes. You can track how you feel each day, view your mood history, and even add your own personal quotes for inspiration.

The project was built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to support mental health and self-reflection in a lightweight, user-friendly way.
